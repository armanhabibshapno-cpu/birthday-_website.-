My Dearest Love,

From the way your eyes light up when you're excited, to how you stand by me even when things get tough. From your ridiculous plans that somehow always work out, to your stubborn hope that refuses to let go even in the darkest moments. Every piece of you is something I've fallen in love with.

I've organized these 100 reasons into different chapters — your personality, your heart, the way you love me, and simply... the magic of you being yourself. Each one is a piece of the puzzle that makes you the most incredible person I know.

So here they are — 100 reasons, 100 truths, 100 little pieces of my heart laid bare for you to see. I hope as you read them, you feel even a fraction of the love I feel for you every single day.

Happy Birthday, my love. Here's to a hundred more reasons, a hundred more years, and a love that keeps growing.

Forever Yours,
